Meet Cassie Betts: Tech Boss

Talk Tech To Me- Uncut is what gets us excited about the new world of technology and features Black innovators who are making waves in the tech world. This highly anticipated podcast is geared toward the inevitable and growing diversity movement in all tech industries. Cassie Betts will spotlight dynamic Black & Brown minds from around the world. Cassie is establishing her third successful tech company in South Los Angeles, MARTY and her non-profit MISLA (Made in South LA) has quickly become the hourglass's narrowest point for youth in tech education. What will Cassie Betts' next project be? To find out, tune in live to Talk Tech To Me, Saturdays at 12:00 pm on KBLA Talk 1580.
